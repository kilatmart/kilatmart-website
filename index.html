How it works

From tap to doorstep in four steps

Step 1

Browse & order

Open the KilatMart app, pick your store, add items to cart, and checkout in seconds.

Step 2

Store packs

Partner store staff pick and pack your order fresh — and if something's out of stock, they can suggest a Smart Substitute for you to approve in-app.

Step 3

Rider picks up

A verified KilatMart rider collects your order and heads your way with GPS tracking.

Step 4

Delivered

OTP-verified handover with photo proof. Enjoy your essentials — rate us if you love the speed!

New · Built into the app

Out of stock? Smart Substitutes keep your order kilat.

Real stores run out of shelves — that's normal. KilatMart doesn't leave you guessing. When an item isn't available, the store proposes a substitute right in your tracking screen. Tap accept to swap, remove the item, or let the countdown auto-accept so packing never stalls.

Store suggests a swap

While picking your order, partner staff propose a like-for-like alternative when an item is unavailable — straight from the store portal.

You decide in seconds

Get a live alert on order tracking — accept the substitute, remove the item, or chat with the store. Clear pricing, no surprises.

Order keeps moving

Once you respond, packing continues and your rider pickup stays on schedule — because quick-commerce should never pause for one empty shelf.

Fewer cancelled items. Faster deliveries. Happier customers.
